Labs are **experimental and fast moving models** available for a limited amount of time.  
We provide access to these models for developers and the community to try them out and provide feedback, allowing us to iterate faster and improve them!

If you are interested in our Labs models for enterprise use cases, please [reach out to us](https://mistral.ai/contact)

Labs are indentifed by the `Labs` tag in the model card and the `labs-` prefix in the model name identifier.  
For example: `labs-devstral-small-2512`

As lab models, we iterate and may update them frequently - they are subject to change and **may be deprecated or removed with 2 weeks' notice.**

We do not recommend using these models in production environments. However, you are welcome to explore them and share your experience.

We greatly appreciate your feedback on our labs models. Please share your thoughts and insights on the [Mistral AI Discord](https://discord.gg/mistralai), the [Mistral AI subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/MistralAI/) or other channels.
