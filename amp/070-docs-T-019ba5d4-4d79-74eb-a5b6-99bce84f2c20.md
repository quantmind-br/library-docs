## PostHog Invitation Entity

The invitation system centers on **OrganizationInvite** which links invitees to organizations:

**Flow**: `OrganizationInvite` → (user accepts) → `OrganizationMembership` created → invite deleted

**Key relationships**:

- **Organization** has many invites and memberships
- **User** creates invites (via `created_by`) and becomes member on acceptance
- **Team** receives granular access via `private_project_access` JSON field
- Invites expire after **3 days** and support role levels: MEMBER (1), ADMIN (8), OWNER (15)
