These are the minimum distribution requirements for approved Spotify Connect partners. Please ensure that you follow them closely.

## Box Requirements

### Spotify logo

Where other music service logos (e.g. YouTube, Pandora, Tidal etc.) are included, add the Spotify service logo on all outer boxes at least as prominently in size and positioning as the most prominent other service logo. Logo use must comply with our [Logo & Colour Guidelines](https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/design).

The Spotify service logo and icon are available to download from [Design Resources](https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/design#using-our-logo).

### Spotify Connect logo

Add the [Spotify Connect compatibility logo](https://spotify.app.box.com/v/spotify-connect-branding) on all outer boxes, at least as prominently in size and positioning as the most prominent other compatibility logo (e.g. Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, AirPlay, Chromecast Built-In, Amazon Voice Service, etc.). Logo use must comply with our [Spotify Connect Logo Guidelines](https://developer-assets.spotifycdn.com/legal/hardware-partners/spotify-connect-logo-guidelines.pdf).

## User Manual Requirements

### Spotify logo

Where other music service logos (e.g. YouTube, Pandora, Tidal etc.) are included, there must be a reference to Spotify in the physical or digital product manual accompanied by the Spotify service logo. Logo use must comply with our [Logo & Colour Guidelines](https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/design).

The Spotify service logo and icon are available to download from [Design Resources](https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/design).

### Spotify Connect logo

Add the [Spotify Connect compatibility logo](https://spotify.app.box.com/v/spotify-connect-branding) in the product manual, at least as prominently in size and positioning as the most prominent other compatibility logo (e.g. Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, AirPlay, Chromecast Built-In, Amazon Voice Service, etc.). Logo use must comply with our [Spotify Connect Logo Guidelines](https://developer.spotify.com/legal/hardware-partners/spotify-connect-logo-guidelines.pdf).

### Third party licenses text

Include the following sentence in the product manual:

*The Spotify Software is subject to third party licenses found here: [](https://www.spotify.com/connect/third-party-licenses)[https://www.spotify.com/connect/third-party-licenses](https://www.spotify.com/connect/third-party-licenses).*

You can find translations for this sentence [here](https://spotify.app.box.com/s/peetk9jouueypz5qag6i6pu0p4i6nie9).

### Instructions on how to use Spotify Connect

Your device instructions manual must include these [instructions](https://spotify.box.com/s/7m8g1ij260oxt8zlhy5cult0gmkknu6t) for how to use Spotify.

If the language is not represented in the list, you can write your own translation, as long as you stay as close as possible to its intended message. Share your translation with us for approval by submitting a certification support ticket in the Spotify Helpdesk.

## Stickers Requirements

The usage of the Spotify Connect compatibility product sticker is voluntary, but becomes required where other compatibility logos are present on the product in box or at retail. In those cases the placement of the Spotify Connect logo should be at least as prominently as any other compatibility logos.

The Spotify Connect logo can be downloaded from the [Spotify Connect Branding page](https://spotify.app.box.com/v/spotify-connect-branding). You can further read about the guidelines on the [Spotify Connect logo guidelines guide](https://developer-assets.spotifycdn.com/legal/hardware-partners/spotify-connect-logo-guidelines.pdf).
