## Product

Amp is the frontier coding agent, and a new way of working. Highly opinionated tooling on top of the best models, squeezing out the last mile of quality. You focus on building. Threads capture the real work: what happens before the commit. Share them with your workspace or the world.

## Company

We're an independent agent research lab. Our goal: learn what the future of software development looks like. We believe the tools, the codebases, how code is reviewed and read: it will all change, fundamentally. Agentic programming is just the start. We ship to find out what's next. Not papers. Product. Exploring the absurd, to find the possible.
