Mistral AI models can be **self-deployed on your own infrastructure** through various inference engines.

## Recommended

[vLLM](https://vllm.readthedocs.io/) — highly-optimized Python-only serving framework which can expose an OpenAI-compatible API.

## Alternatives

- [TensorRT-LLM](https://github.com/NVIDIA/TensorRT-LLM)
- [TGI](https://huggingface.co/docs/text-generation-inference/index)

## Infrastructure Management

- [SkyPilot](https://skypilot.readthedocs.io)
- [Cerebrium](https://www.cerebrium.ai)

## Enterprise

For full-stack enterprise deployment, from efficient model inference to team management, we recommend [reaching out to us](https://mistral.ai/contact).
