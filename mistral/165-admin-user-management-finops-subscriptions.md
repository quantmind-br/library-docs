# Subscriptions

Manage your subscription plan from the Admin Panel. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time.

## Available Plans

| Plan | Features |
|------|----------|
| **Free** | Core features with daily usage limits |
| **Pro** | Higher usage limits for individual power users |
| **Student** | Pro features at a reduced rate for students and educators |
| **Team** | Pro features plus role-based access, centralized billing, and enterprise Connectors |
| **Enterprise** | Team features plus SAML SSO, audit logs, custom deployment, and SLA support |

Annual billing is available for Pro and Team plans at a discounted rate compared to monthly. For current pricing, see the [pricing page](https://mistral.ai/pricing).

## Upgrading

1. Open [Admin Subscriptions](https://admin.mistral.ai/subscriptions).
2. Click **Compare plans** to view options.
3. Select your plan and billing frequency.
4. Enter billing information.
5. Confirm payment.

Payment is due immediately and at the start of each billing period.

Accepted payment methods: credit card and Google Pay.

## Downgrading or Canceling

1. Open [Admin Subscriptions](https://admin.mistral.ai/subscriptions).
2. Click **Unsubscribe** in the Downgrade section.

You keep full access until the end of the current billing period, then your account reverts to the Free plan. Click **Resume subscription** to restore your plan before the period ends.

## Refunds

> [!warning]
> Refunds are available only within **14 days of initial purchase**. Contact [support](https://help.mistral.ai) to request a refund within this window.

## Enterprise

For Enterprise plans with SAML SSO, audit logs, custom deployment, and SLA support, contact [sales@mistral.ai](mailto:sales@mistral.ai).
