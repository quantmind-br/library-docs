## Agenda[​](#agenda "Direct link to Agenda")

1. Welcome and brief on format
2. Quick individual introductions if the size of the group allows
3. Opportunity for people to pitch ideas to talk about (30 seconds)
4. Sort the list of ideas in terms of interest (talks with prepared demos / slides are likely to be prioritised)
5. Run the sessions
