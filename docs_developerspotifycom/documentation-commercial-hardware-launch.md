Before launching a product with Spotify Connect, all devices need to get certified by Spotify and each partner needs a signed distribution agreement with Spotify.

Please do the following:

**1**. [**Test and submit a certification**](https://certomato.spotify.com) using Spotify’s self-testing certification tool Certomato.

**2**. **Send Spotify two test devices**. Deliver two launch-ready test devices or model variants incorporating the Spotify Connect integration, at no charge to Spotify, including all fees associated with shipment for certification, such as duties and taxes:

*Attn: Hardware Partners, Spotify Regeringsgatan 19, 111 53 Stockholm, Sweden*

- Ensure that the setup and firmware update procedures for the device are platform-agnostic.
- Please provide your company phone number to ensure you can be contacted directly if anything happens during shipping.

**3**. **Get certification approval** from Spotify.

**4**. **Sign the distribution agreement** and get confirmation from Spotify (see [distribution](https://developer.spotify.com/documentation/commercial-hardware/launch/distribution) section).

Thats it!
