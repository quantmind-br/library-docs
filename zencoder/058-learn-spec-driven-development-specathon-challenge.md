## Submission checklist

- ✅ Built with Zencoder agents or workflows (call out which ones).
- ✅ Attach or link to the real spec document (any readable format).
- ✅ Include a screenshot that shows the main experience in action.
- ✅ Add a short note describing the feature, repo/environment, and beneficiary.

## Submit everything

Email your materials to [specathon@zencoder.ai](mailto:specathon@zencoder.ai?subject=Specathon.com%20Challenge%20Submission) with the subject **“Specathon.com Challenge Submission.”** If anything is missing, we’ll follow up before judging.

## Terms & credit details

Review the full [Specathon.com Challenge Terms](https://zencoder.ai/lp/specathon-terms) for eligibility rules, the one-winner-per-week policy, and how credits are applied.
