## Sign distribution agreement

Once you have passed certification you will get a distribution agreement to sign. After signing you are good to go and can launch your product with Spotify Connect! The distribution agreement is mandatory but only has to be done once per organization.

## Explore Spotify

If your device has passed the certification, Spotify would like to include your product on [Explore Spotify](https://explore.spotify.com). Spotify will create a listing of your product after the product has been certified.

Please note: The product will not go live on Explore Spotify until we have explicit confirmation from you that the product has launched in the market.
