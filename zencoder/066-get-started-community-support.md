## Join Our Communities

Becoming part of the Zencoder community is one of the best ways to maximize your experience with our tools. Our community spaces are where you’ll find:

- **Early Updates** - Be the first to hear about new features, events, and improvements
- **Team Interaction** - Connect directly with Zencoder team members
- **Peer Support** - Learn from other developers using Zencoder in their workflows
- **Knowledge Sharing** - Exchange tips, tricks, and best practices
- **Networking** - Build relationships with like-minded developers

## Why Join Our Communities?

Our community spaces are more than just support channels—they’re vibrant ecosystems where:

- **Problems get solved faster** through collective knowledge
- **Your voice shapes the product** as we actively listen to community discussions
- **You’ll discover new workflows** from peers using Zencoder in different contexts
- **You can showcase your projects** and get constructive feedback
- **You’ll be the first to know** about beta features, events, and opportunities

## Community Standards

We’re committed to maintaining welcoming, inclusive spaces where everyone can learn and contribute. To keep our communities helpful and respectful, we ask all members to follow these guidelines: **What We Encourage:**

- Helpful, constructive discussions about development and Zencoder
- Sharing knowledge, tips, and best practices
- Respectful questions and feedback
- Supporting fellow community members

**What We Don’t Allow:**

- Hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Offensive, inappropriate, or disrespectful content
- Self-promotion, spam, or promotional links
- Off-topic discussions that derail conversations
- Sharing personal information or doxxing
- Trolling, flame wars, or intentionally disruptive behavior

We believe great communities are built on mutual respect and shared purpose. Thank you for helping us create spaces where developers can thrive together!

Your feedback is invaluable to us and directly influences our development roadmap. We’ve made it easy to share your thoughts:

[**Submit Feedback**

Our dedicated feedback form helps us understand your needs and prioritize improvements](https://zencoder.ai/feedback/in-trial)

### Why Your Feedback Matters

When you share feedback with us:

- It goes directly to our product and engineering teams
- It helps us identify patterns in user needs
- It influences our feature prioritization
- It helps us refine existing capabilities

We regularly review all feedback and use it to guide our development cycles, ensuring Zencoder continues to evolve in ways that matter most to you.
