> [!warning]
> Labs are experimental, fast-moving models available for a limited time. **Not recommended for production.**

Labs provide access for developers and community to try models and provide feedback, enabling faster iteration.

For enterprise interest: [contact Mistral AI](https://mistral.ai/contact)

## Identifying Labs Models

- **Tag in model card**: `Labs`
- **Name prefix**: `labs-`

Example: `labs-devstral-small-2512`

## Lifecycle

Labs models may be **deprecated or removed with 2 weeks' notice**.
