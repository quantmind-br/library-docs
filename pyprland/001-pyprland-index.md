## Customizable

## Fast and easy

Designed for performance and simplicity

It's a software that extends the functionality of your desktop environment (Hyprland, Niri, etc...), adding new features and improving the existing ones.

It also enables a high degree of customization and automation, making it easier to adapt to your workflow.

To understand the potential of Pyprland, you can check the [plugins](https://hyprland-community.github.io/pyprland/Plugins.html) page.

## Major recent changes [​](#major-recent-changes)

- Major rewrite of the [Monitors plugin](https://hyprland-community.github.io/pyprland/monitors.html) delivers improved stability and functionality.
- The [Wallpapers plugin](https://hyprland-community.github.io/pyprland/wallpapers.html) now applies [rounded corners](https://hyprland-community.github.io/pyprland/wallpapers.html#radius) per display and derives cohesive [color schemes from the background](https://hyprland-community.github.io/pyprland/wallpapers.html#templates) (Matugen/Pywal-inspired).
