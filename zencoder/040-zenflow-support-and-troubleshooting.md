## Getting Support

If you encounter issues with Zenflow, contact our support team at [support@zencoder.ai](mailto:support@zencoder.ai).

## Log Files Location

Zenflow stores all log files locally on your machine. Logs are never transmitted automatically and remain under your control. **macOS**: `~/Library/Application Support/zenflow-desktop/logs/` **Windows**: `C:\Users\pc\AppData\Roaming\zenflow-desktop\logs`

## What to Include When Reporting Issues

When contacting support, include the following to help us resolve your issue quickly:

- **App version and build**: Find this in **Settings → About** or **Help → About Zenflow** (e.g., `Zenflow Desktop v1.2.3 build 456`)
- **Operating system**: Include version and architecture (e.g., `macOS 14.2.1 Sonoma - Apple Silicon M2` or `Windows 11 Pro 23H2 x64`)
- **Timestamps and timezone**: When did the issue occur? (e.g., `December 16, 2025 at 2:30 PM EST`)
- **Steps to reproduce**: Describe what you were doing when the error occurred and whether it happens consistently
- **Screenshots or recordings**: Capture error messages, frozen screens, or unexpected behavior
- **Log files**: Attach relevant log files from the locations above
- **Network details**: Are you using a VPN, proxy, or behind a firewall? Include any network restrictions that might affect connectivity
