ForgeCode is the world’s top-ranked coding harness, leading TermBench 2.0 and setting a new state of the art.

### Built atop ZSH

Type \`:\` to talk to ForgeCode right inside your terminal. Your custom aliases and Oh My Zsh plugins keep working — ForgeCode slots in without friction.

[Learn More](https://forgecode.dev/docs/zsh-support)

### Codebase Understanding

ForgeCode Services brings a powerful context engine that navigates huge codebases, super fast tool corrections that keeps local models on track, and scales over thousands of skills — all without bloating your context window.

[ForgeCode Services](https://forgecode.dev/docs/forge-services)

### Pick the right model for every task

Use a thinking model to plan, a fast model to code, and a large-context model for big files. ForgeCode lets you mix and match without restarting your session.
