We heard you! [Amp Free](https://ampcode.com/news/amp-free) no longer requires you to opt into training. Now more programmers can use Amp Free as a free coding agent for both sensitive work code and on personal side projects.

How is this possible?

It turns out that the Internet's best business model (advertising) works, and we're able to make Amp Free without the training requirement.

Try it: [install or update Amp](https://ampcode.com/manual#getting-started), then use `/mode free` in the Amp CLI, or toggle `smart`/`free` mode in the editor extension.

Thank you to our fantastic launch advertisers who are making free agentic coding possible:

![Amp Free Launch Partners](https://ampcode.com/news/launch-partners-light.svg)

The `free` mode now meets all of the stringent [security standards](https://ampcode.com/security) of Amp's paid `smart` mode.

If you're already using Amp Free, we've switched [training mode](https://ampcode.com/security#model-training) off for you because it's no longer required.

Workspace admins can enable or disable Amp Free for all workspace members in [workspace settings](https://ampcode.com/settings). If enabled (the default), workspace members will see the `smart`/`free` mode toggle. Amp Free is not currently available in Enterprise workspaces.
